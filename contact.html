New Technology, Experienced Hands

Malcolm Connolly

CEO

Malcolm Connolly has been using drones to push the boundaries of industrial inspection in the energy industry since 2008, when he founded Cyberhawk. He pioneered the use of drones in the marine industry, conducting the world's first Class approved (DNV) drone survey in 2015 on an FPSO - a project which won the Oil & Gas UK Award for Business Efficiency. In 2017, Malcolm carried out the first USCG approved CAIP survey using a drone and in 2018, the first class approved (ABS) special IV survey on an ESP crude carrier.

Salvis Bralitis

Operations Director

Over the last decade, Sal has been managing complex projects and developing inspection solutions for the energy industry. As a graduate civil engineer, Sal joined Cyberhawk as the first inspection engineer outside the board. After eight years at Cyberhawk, Sal joined tech startup, Aerones, where he took active role in robotic inspection system development, consolidation and field testing. Sal has completed over 500 inspection projects and has led teams internationally throughout his 10+ years in the drone and robotics sector.
