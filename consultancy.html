Consultancy

Training, consultancy and operational mentoring

The C-Bird team have a combined 30+ years’ experience in developing innovative inspection techniques, implementing surveying technologies and building enterprise-wide drone programmes.

We’ve built the gold standard, and now we’re sharing our experience with you.

For Hardware Developers

Taking a drone solution successfully from concept to deployment in harsh industrial environments is a serious challenge. C-Bird has a proven track record of successfully working with leading drone hardware manufacturers. We help engineering teams focus their product development resources on the most important features and capabilities. C-Bird’s experience and significant operational expertise can also be called upon to support early-stage field-trialling of prototypes in industrial environments.

For Asset Owners

Building a drone programme for conducting your own inspections can be challenging without appropriate guidance and previous knowledge. C-Bird can close those gaps for drone technology adopters, by bringing in our wide-ranging expertise in drone operations.

For Drone Operations

We can help create maximum value from your drone program by assessing and recommending the right equipment for your operations. We can also develop operations manuals, secure flight permissions, create safety frameworks, devise internal company training programs and advise on equipment maintenance regimes.

For Training Managers

If you’d like to elevate your drone operations and get the most out of your personnel, we have the course for you. Our instructors will come to your location and provide a four-day theoretical and practical training course. By the end, you’ll boost efficiency and productivity without sacrificing safety.
